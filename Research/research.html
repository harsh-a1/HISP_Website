Research & Publications

HISP INDIA provides services around a suite of health informatics products for public health sector. We contribute in capacity building and implementation support, research, a PhD program,and hosting the core DHIS2 software development team.


Assessment of the ability of the Health Management Information System in India to use information for action.


A mini-thesis submitted in partial fulfillment of the requirements for the degree of Master of Public Health at the School of Public Health, University of the Western Cape.The primary aim of the thesis was to assess the existing Health Management Information System (HMIS) in India with respect to its ability to support the use of information for action in priority areas identified by the national and state governments.
Date: July 2011
